5 Significant Artworks in Philippines Technological History
3. Augmented Reality in History (Gateway Gallery)

Artist:
The Gateway Gallery project was developed through collaboration between Filipino artists, historians, and institutions like the University of the Philippines.

Meaning of the artwork:
This combines traditional painting with modern technology, allowing viewers to experience history through digital interaction.

Cultural significance:
The use of augmented reality in art represents a major shift in how Filipinos engage with history and culture. Instead of simply observing, viewers become active participants in learning. This innovation helps make historical knowledge more accessible, especially to younger generations who are more familiar with digital technology. It also ensures that important historical narratives remain relevant in the digital age. By blending tradition with innovation, it shows how Filipino culture continues to evolve while preserving its past.

About

The history of technology is the story of how humans create tools and systems to solve problems and improve their way of life. In the Philippines, this history begins with early inventions such as stone tools, pottery, and weaving, which helped ancient communities survive and adapt to their environment.

As time passed, technology developed further through trade, colonization, and cultural exchange, leading to advancements in transportation, communication, and industry.

In the modern and digital age, Filipino inventors and innovators continue to contribute to technology through medical devices, communication systems, and sustainable solutions.

The history of technology shows how creativity, knowledge, and necessity drive innovation. It also highlights how technology is closely connected to culture, shaping the way people live, communicate, and express themselves across different periods of time.

5 Significant Artworks in Philippines Technological History
2. “Rising Philippines” Mural (Modernization in Art)

Artist:
The mural was created by Victorio Edades, Carlos Francisco, and Galo Ocampo.

Meaning of the artwork:
The mural symbolizes national progress, showing the Philippines moving from traditional life to modernization, including technology like ships and airplanes.

Cultural significance:
This artwork reflects a crucial period in Philippine history when the country was shaping its identity as a modern nation. It shows how technology and industrialization influenced Filipino life and aspirations. The mural highlights the balance between preserving cultural roots and embracing progress. It also represents national pride and unity, showing how Filipinos viewed modernization as a path toward independence and development. Even today, it reminds viewers of the country’s journey through colonization, change, and growth.
